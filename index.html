About me


I am a webdeveloper and i love coding. I want to share with you the processes of creating designs and implementing code. I just want to inspire all the curious learners to experiment with designs and understand how designs speak, I plan to achieve this by offering designs with an actual idea at its core using HTML, CSS and Javascript.
I am a continuous learner. Learning for me equates to exploring new ideas and growing as an individual. I just cannot imagine my life devoid of designing. I freely express my creativity and imagination by designing. And to give my passion of designing a platform I have a YouTube channel named, Coder_Z. Subscribe to my channel and strap in and enjoy the ride of creating and coding. Thank you, and I hope you have as much fun watching and learning from my videos as much as I have making them for you !
